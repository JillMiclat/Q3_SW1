CLUELESS CHARACTERS

Cherilyn "Cher" Horowitz

Cher is a wealthy and attractive girl who is kind-hearted, sweet, and popular. She is the social queen of Bronson Alcott High School and lives in a Beverly Hills mansion with her adoring but tough-as-nails lawyer father, whose deal-making and negotiating abilities she inherited.

Dionne Davenport

Cher's best friend Dionne aka dee is also beautiful, popular, and wealthy. She is Cher's chief fashion critic as well as a caring and genuine supporter. She's intelligent, level-headed, and social. Dionne is independent and somewhat feminist. She is not afraid to call her boyfriend out when he says something stupid.

Tai Frasier

She used to be a drug addict who doesn't care about high school, but under Cher and Dionne's guidance, she learns the ins and outs of social status. Tai's confidence rises after a change in hair, make-up, and clothing, and she becomes at ease with the popular clique.

Amber Mariens

Popular Girls can't be friend with everyone, Amber is Cher's nemesis at the school. Cher compares Amber to a Monet painting, saying, "from afar, it's okay, but up close, it's a big old mess." Amber constantly competes with Cher in all areas, from style and fashion to debate class to popularity and boys.

Christian

A well-dressed and intriguing new student at school. He dresses like a Rat Pack member, enjoys modern sculpture, has "a thing for Tony Curtis," and is Cher's ideal shopping companion.

Josh Lucas

Cher's ex-stepbrother is ambitious and socially conscious, with aspirations of becoming an environmental lawyer. He is portrayed as a bleeding-heart liberal who listens to alternative rock, reads the news, and is unconcerned with superficialities. He appears to think Cher is frivolous and selfish at first, but as the film progresses, he reveals a more protective side and shows caring and concern for her.
